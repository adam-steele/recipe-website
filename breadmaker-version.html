Seeded loaf (breadmaker version)

Time: 4hr


Servings: 1 loaves (10 slices per loaf)

Tips & Tricks
  • If baking without breadmaker, safer to add slightly less liquid intially and more later if needed

  • Whilst the amount of flour needs to be 500g in total. You can add more Wholemeal if preferred, for different flavour & texture

  • Seeds can also be substitued by this is my recommendaition. A mix of sizes is best
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 500g
  • 300g Strong Bread Flour
  • 200g Wholemeal Flour
Salt 1tsp
Fast acting yeast 7g dried
Mixed seeds 1tbsp each of,
  • Seasame
  • Pumpkin
  • Sunflower
Water 350ml
Olive oil 4tbsp


Step 1: Add 500g flour to bradmaker tin.

Step 2: Add 1 tsp salt.

Step 3: Add 7g of dried yeast. (best to measure in smaller thing such as cup as accidentally adding excess not good)

Step 4: Add 1 tbsp of each seed.

Step 5: Mix to combine, then add 350ml water. This should be warm to the touch, to start the reaction, but not so hot as to kill yeast.

Step 6: Add 4 tbsp olive oil. Mix well, this starts the proving process which may not work properly in breadmaker otherwise.

Step 7: Place tin back in breadmaker.

Step 8: Settings should be bake, med color crust and and large size.

Step 9: When taking out needs 30 mins too cool before eating on a wire rack.
